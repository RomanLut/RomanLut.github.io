**FinConPro and MyLifePlan**


**FinConPro** is a Swiss all-in-one financial advisory software that combines CRM, wealth management, and pension and investment planning, designed for banks, advisors, and insurance professionals. It supports holistic financial planning, customer onboarding, and lifecycle investment analysis in a compliant business platform. 
**MyLifePlan** is the individual-focused planning component that works with FinConPro to help create personalized long-term financial and life goals.

**Platform:** HTML5
**Language:** Typescript, Angular framework
**My role:** Frontend developer
